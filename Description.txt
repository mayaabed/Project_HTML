We will create a music player with a user interface that can be used to play music in the browser.
Functionalities:
1. Upload a track with its src property
2. Button play
3. Button pause
4. Button nextTrack
5. Button prevTrack
6. Show the current time playing of a track
7. Control the volume
8. Search track 
9. Click on track-cover and go to the Youtube music-video
10. Control the current time playing of a track
